Get Started in Minutes

Install the Expo build

Load the latest APK or development build onto your device or emulator.

Enter student credentials

Open the in-app dashboard, add your username and password, then tap save to sync.

Pin the widget

Long-press your Android home screen, pick “YRC Canteen Widget”, and drop it where it belongs.

Refresh anytime

Hit refresh or enable auto updates to stay on top of your cafeteria spending before lunch rush.
